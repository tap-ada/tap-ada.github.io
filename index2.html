FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about staking with TAPSY.

Is my ADA locked when I stake with TAPSY?

No. Your ADA always stays in your own wallet. You can move or undelegate it at any time; staking only assigns your stake to the pool for rewards.

How are the pool fees applied?

The pool receives a fixed fee of 340 ADA per epoch plus the configured margin (0 %). The fee is taken from the pool's total rewards before the remainder is distributed to delegators.[web:2][web:35]

When do I receive my first rewards?

After you delegate, it takes about 2–3 epochs before your delegation becomes active and rewards start to appear in your wallet.

Is it risky to choose a smaller pool?

Smaller pools can be less saturated and offer good decentralization, but need sufficient stake (roughly 1–2 M ADA) to mint blocks reliably.[web:35]
